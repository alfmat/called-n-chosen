About Us

Called and Chosen is a Catholic Evangelization Ministry. The Diocese of Raleigh’s Office of Evangelization and Discipleship provides us with formation, support and promotion. We strive to promulgate awareness of Catholic teachings and precepts, strengthen faith, impart Christian values and charity, through evangelization workshops, retreats, fellowship, prayer and reflection of Scriptures.

Brief Intro.

Mission Statement

Guiding Values

Our Mission is to help the common man, be it the Unaffiliated (or the Nones), the Fallen-away Catholics or the non-Christians, to find renewed hope and meaning in life through the teachings and precepts of Catholicism.

How We Can Help

Our Commitment to You

We offer monthly workshops that offer direction and sense of purpose to people seeking hope amidst life's myriad challenges. Amongst others, we attempt to answer key questions around:

  • How can I grapple with situations around me? Am I alone in my struggles?
  • Is there hope in life? How can I fathom the creation around me?
  • Is it mere happenstance? Or is there intelligent design or a creator behind creation?
  • What does this mean for me in my life and my current situation?
  • What is life's purpose? Is there such a thing called salvation? What lies beyond life?
  • Can I find hope, peace and joy in this life? How and where can I explore answers to the above?

Upcoming Events

Add Them to Your Calendar!

Our Workshop Structure consists of a 1-day Program (Third Saturday/Sunday of every month) eligible for anyone between Ages 20-65. For Nones, Atheists, Agnostics, Fallen-away Christians / Catholics and People of Other Faiths:

  • Why God (Common themes – e.g. beauty, intelligent design /creation)
  • Is there a creator (Father) behind this intelligent design?
  • The concept of the creator – a Trinitarian God?
  • Christ amongst other Gods (Apologetics)
  • Why Christ and Christianity (our path to salvation thru Christ)
  • Christian Music – Praise and Worship (Whoever sings prays twice)
  • Why be Catholic? – (Jesus, Sacraments, Apostolic tradition, the Church)
  • Why is it important to be saved? –Daily saving grace -the Sacraments
  • Holy Spirit – As our helper, our counselor, leading us to Christ
  • Immediate Next Steps: The Encounter in Holy Mass; Fellowship
  • Learn about signing up for the RCIA at a local Church
  • Additional Avenues: Renewal Retreat. Prayer Meetings, Fellowship gatherings etc.
