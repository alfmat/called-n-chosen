  • 2017-2019

    Our Humble Beginnings

    We started as a small prayer group in a local Catholic church within the Diocese of Raleigh.

  • March 2019

    We evolved into the Life in Christ Prayer Group

    Prayer Group members now comprise parishioners from multiple Catholic Churches within the Diocese of Raleigh.

  • September 2019

    First Diocesan Retreat in Raleigh

    Life in Christ prayer group is instrumental in working with members of the diocese to bring the first diocesan retreat focused on evangelization and the Charisms of the Holy Spirit.

  • July 2020

    Expansion into Evangelization

    Life in Christ expands scope to address Evangelization by forming Called and Chosen Catholic Evangelization ministry, with support from the Diocese of Raleigh - Office of Evangelization.
